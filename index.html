Mangata and Gallo is a jewelry store that specializes in special occasions like engagements, weddings and anniversaries.

Who We Are

We primarily operate online and have a small storefront in Austin, Texas with an atelier attached for browsing.

Best in Class

Mangata & Gallo's selection of jewelry is known for its high-quality and classic look. The owner, an Austin local, is well known for her jewelry designs.

Meet the Creator

Mariana is the owner and the lead designer of Mangata and Gallo. After graduating from design school with a specialization in diamond cutting and metal smithing, Mariana opened a store in her hometown.
